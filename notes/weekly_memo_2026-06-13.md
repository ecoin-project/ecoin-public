This means the visualization and the statistical comparison are not using an equally meaningful temporal alignment.

Before making any alignment claim, the pipeline should probably use one of the following:

* weekly resampling of market data
* nearest-date merge
* week-ending date normalization
* lagged weekly windows
* rolling correlation after enough weekly points accumulate

Until then, the alignment report should remain labeled as provisional.

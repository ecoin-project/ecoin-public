# Scoring rubric (provisional)

## Scale
Use a provisional 0-100 scale.

- 0-20 = very weak / barely present
- 21-40 = low
- 41-60 = moderate
- 61-80 = strong
- 81-100 = very strong / dominant

## Candidate dimensions

### fear_intensity
How strongly fear, threat, risk, danger, or loss language appears.

### superiority_intensity
How strongly discourse is framed through hierarchy, inferiority, superiority, replacement, or status comparison.

### delegated_agency_intensity
How strongly solutions are framed as outsourcing judgment, action, or control to external systems or agents.

### exploration_score
How much discourse remains open-ended, curious, or possibility-seeking rather than closed and defensive.

### expansion_score
How strongly discourse pushes toward adoption, scale, acceleration, or growth.

### fixation_score
How strongly discourse narrows into repetitive closure, obsession, panic, or single-track interpretation.

### urgency_score
How strongly language implies immediate action, countdown pressure, or “too late” framing.

## Rule
These scores are provisional sidecar measurements, not canonical observation fields.

## Reading the current pattern

Across the three manual batches, fear remains consistently high, but trends slightly downward from batch 001 to batch 003.

Delegated agency rises across all three batches, suggesting that solution-seeking increasingly takes the form of external systems, monitoring, structure, or guidance.

Exploration declines from batch 001 to batch 002 and only partially recovers in batch 003. This keeps the overall mode closer to "fixation leaning" than to exploratory expansion.

Fixation proxy peaks in batch 002, then eases slightly in batch 003. This suggests that batch 003 still reflects elevated anxiety-management behavior, but with somewhat lower compression than batch 002.

# Crisis Resonance Observation Case

## Purpose

This document defines a non-directive observation case for crisis-driven social, economic, and psychological resonance.

The goal is not to predict war, assign blame, provide investment advice, or evaluate political legitimacy.

The goal is to observe how crisis signals propagate across:
- mobility
- resources
- markets
- discourse
- human state
- trust
- perceived value
- choice contraction

## Boundary

This case must not be used for:
- investment recommendations
- political persuasion
- population surveillance
- individual risk scoring
- war prediction
- blame assignment

## Observation Lanes

1. Event facts
2. Aviation and mobility
3. Energy and logistics
4. Market references
5. Currency and safe-haven movement
6. Social media discourse
7. Human state signals
8. ECOIN state indicators

## ECOIN Indicators

- Meaning concentration
- Choice contraction
- Trust collapse
- Scapegoat concentration
- Urgency pressure
- Safe-haven migration
- Resource anxiety
- Mobility restriction
- Settlement pressure
